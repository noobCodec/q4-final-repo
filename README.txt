Quake 4 Wave + Farming Simulator
Press L on launch for in game Help Screen to see keybinds


How to Play:
First hit X once to clear all enemies and teleport you to the play area
Then hit X again once ready to spawn the first wave
After beating the first wave plant any seeds you find to get guns/ammo/health and more seeds
These are the binds to plant:
H - Sugar Kane
J -	Wheat
K - Tree
When you are ready hit X again to spawn the next wave

The mod also comes with 10 upgrades for the player
hit G in game to open the menu
once you make your selections hit G again to close it and save your upgrades
